# MRI lane — external resources

Curated text + video for the MRI lane. **Videos are curated from search, not
individually vetted** — a shortlist; pick what clicks. Text from mriquestions.com /
PMC / Wikipedia / IMAIOS is higher-trust. Full source record:
[../../../research/deep_dives/2026-06-17_orientation_resources.md](../../../research/deep_dives/2026-06-17_orientation_resources.md).

> **Stuck on the end-to-end pipeline (tube → image)? Start here.**
> [IMAIOS e-MRI](https://www.imaios.com/en/e-mri) — free interactive course. Read
> **chapters 1→5 in order**: (1) Nuclear Magnetic Resonance → (2) Instrumentation &
> Safety → (3) NMR Signal & Contrast (T1/T2, TR/TE) → (4) Spatial Encoding → (5)
> Image Formation (k-space → inverse Fourier). Chapter 13 = Cardiac MRI (= A2).
> Book alternative: *"MRI: From Picture to Proton"* (image-first, beginner-canonical).
