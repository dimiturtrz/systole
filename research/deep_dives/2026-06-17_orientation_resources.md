## Whole-pipeline resource (best for "I don't get the end-to-end flow")
**IMAIOS e-MRI** — free interactive course, 21 chapters. Chapters **1→5 in order =
the complete tube→image pipeline**: (1) Nuclear Magnetic Resonance → (2)
Instrumentation & Safety → (3) NMR Signal & Contrast (T1/T2, TR/TE) → (4) Spatial
Encoding (gradients, slice selection) → (5) Image Formation (k-space, inverse FT).
Chapter 13 = Cardiac MRI (covers A2 later).
- Entry: https://www.imaios.com/en/e-mri
- Image formation: https://www.imaios.com/en/e-mri/mri-image-formation
- Gradients & spatial frequency: https://www.imaios.com/en/e-mri/mri-image-formation/gradients-and-spatial-frequency
- K-space: https://www.imaios.com/en/e-Courses/e-MRI/The-Physics-behind-it-all/K-space
- ⚠ Access: site pushes "SUBSCRIBE"; core article text generally free, some pages/quizzes may gate.

Book alternative (canonical beginner, image-first): **"MRI: From Picture to Proton"**
(McRobbie et al.). Fully-free fallback: mriquestions.com.
