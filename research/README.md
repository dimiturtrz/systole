# research/

Teacher-side grounding behind the `learning/` writeups — the cited raw material I
research before teaching a topic. Rougher than `learning/` (which is the distilled
study version), but public: it shows the sources and reasoning the understanding
rests on.

Format (mirrors how I keep research elsewhere):
- `deep_dives/<date>_<topic>.md` — one focused, cited research pass per topic.
- `summaries/` — cross-topic synthesis when it accumulates.

See [../learning/README.md](../learning/README.md) for the full circuit.
